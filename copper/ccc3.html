About Us

Welcome to Copper Coast Ceramics, where clay meets the captivating spirit of the Irish coastline. Our studio, nestled near the scenic town of Tramore in County Waterford, draws endless inspiration from the rugged cliffs, shifting seas, and rich mineral hues of the Copper Coast, a UNESCO Global Geopark.

Each piece is thoughtfully handcrafted, blending traditional pottery techniques with a contemporary aesthetic. We strive to create ceramics that are not only beautiful and functional but also tell a story of their origin – a tribute to the natural artistry that surrounds us. From our hands to your home, we hope our work brings a touch of Ireland's unique coastal magic into your life.
